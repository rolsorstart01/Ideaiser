Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: December 27, 2025

1. Cancellation Policy

Users can cancel their Pro subscription at any time. To cancel, please contact us at reyhansingh01@gmail.com with your registered email address. Upon cancellation, your Pro benefits will remain active until the end of the current billing cycle.

2. Refund Eligibility

Due to the digital nature of our AI evaluation services and the associated API costs (Google Gemini), we generally do not offer refunds once the service has been accessed. However, refunds may be considered in the following cases:

  • Duplicate Charges: If you were accidentally charged twice for the same billing period.
  • Technical Failure: If a technical issue prevents you from using the service and we are unable to resolve it within 7 business days.

3. Non-Refundable Items

The following are non-refundable:

  • Analyses already performed.
  • Donations made to the project via the "Support" section.
  • Partial month usage.

4. Refund Process

To request a refund, please email reyhansingh01@gmail.com within 48 hours of the transaction. Approved refunds will be processed via Razorpay and may take 5-7 business days to reflect in your account.

5. Custom Donations

Please note that any voluntary donations made through the "Support Ideaiser" section are final and non-refundable, as they are contributions toward project development.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding refunds or cancellations, please reach out to us at reyhansingh01@gmail.com.
