Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 27, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Ideaiser ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

2. Data We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and profile picture when you sign up via Google or Email.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our app, including business ideas submitted for analysis and your interaction with the AI coach.
  • Payment Information: We use Razorpay for payment processing. We do not store your credit card or bank details on our servers.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and maintain our AI evaluation service.
  • Sync your Pro status and analysis history across devices.
  • Communicate with you about your account or service updates.
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our AI scoring models.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud). We implement industry-standard security measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or modification of your data.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information with:

  • Google Gemini API: Your business ideas are sent to Google's AI for evaluation.
  • Firebase/Google Cloud: For hosting and database management.
  • Razorpay: To process payments and subscriptions.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can manage your account settings or contact us to request data deletion.
